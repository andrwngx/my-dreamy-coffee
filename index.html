Dreamy Cafe - Our History

Dreamy Cafe opened in Hanoi, Vietnam's capital, in November 2022. Having missed the coffee taste or simply the cafe atmosphere during the COVID-19 Pandemic, I decided to open Dreamy Cafe immediately after the pandemic ended. Everyone starts their day at Dreamy Cafe, where they can enjoy the best coffee cups and cozy cafe ambiance!
